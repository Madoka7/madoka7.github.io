Brief Biography

Xiangqi Kong received his B.S. degree in Network Engineering in 2013, from Dalian University of Technology. Between 2014 and 2015, he received Learning Merit Scholarship (top 20 percent). Currently he is a PhD student in University of Rhode Island advised by Prof. Bin Li.
